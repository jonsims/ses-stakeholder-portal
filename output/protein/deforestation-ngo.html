Who You Represent

What's at Stake
  • JBS's demonstrated failure to meet its 2025 deforestation pledge

  • Expansion of soy in the unprotected Cerrado

Your Priorities & Evidence
  • Ending deforestation linked to beef and feed crop supply chains
  • Achieving binding, traceable zero-deforestation commitments from major buyers
  • Redirecting capital toward protein transition
  • Holding corporations legally and reputationally accountable for supply chain harms
  • Strengthening EU Deforestation Regulation and equivalent US policies
