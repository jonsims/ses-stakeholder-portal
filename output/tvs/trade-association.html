Consumer Technology Association VP

Vice President of Government Affairs — Consumer Technology Association (CTA) or equivalent industry trade body

Who You Represent

The collective interests of the consumer electronics industry in the US and internationally, including TV brands, component manufacturers, and retailers; CTA is North America's largest technology trade association, representing companies that support over 18 million US jobs.

What's at Stake
  • Mandatory supply chain due diligence legislation that imposes compliance costs and legal liability
  • Tariffs on Chinese panels and components that raise TV prices for US consumers
  • Proposed regulations on hazardous chemical disclosure
  • Any regulatory move toward mandatory minimum product lifespan or software support periods that would constrain product design decisions.
Your Priorities & Evidence
  • Maintaining market access for global supply chains
  • Advocating for voluntary rather than mandatory sustainability standards
  • Opposing tariffs that raise component costs
  • Promoting industry-led recycling initiatives over mandatory extended producer responsibility schemes
  • Representing the industry's position that efficiency improvements (energy per screen area down 76% since 2003) demonstrate responsible self-governance.
