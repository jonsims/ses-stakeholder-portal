Who You Represent

Vertically integrated consumer electronics corporations that design, market, and (in part) manufacture televisions; responsible to shareholders, boards, and increasingly to ESG-focused institutional investors.

What's at Stake
  • Disruption to rare earth or indium supply from Chinese export controls
  • Reputational damage from conflict mineral exposure in the DRC supply chain
  • Rising panel procurement costs
  • Competitive disadvantage relative to Chinese brands with cheaper, state-subsidized panel supply
  • Regulatory compliance costs for supply chain due diligence.
Your Priorities & Evidence
  • Maintaining cost-competitive material supply chains
  • Protecting brand reputation from adverse sustainability headlines
  • Meeting voluntary ESG commitments and mandatory supply chain disclosure requirements (e.g., EU Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive)
  • Securing preferential access to critical materials (indium, rare earths) as geopolitical supply risk rises.
